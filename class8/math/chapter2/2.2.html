1. \( \frac{x}{2} - \frac{1}{5} = \frac{x}{3} + \frac{1}{4} \)
Transpose \( \frac{x}{3} \) to LHS and \( \frac{1}{5} \) to RHS:
\( \frac{x}{2} - \frac{x}{3} = \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{5} \)
Take LCMs: LHS is \( \frac{3x - 2x}{6} = \frac{x}{6} \); RHS is \( \frac{5+4}{20} = \frac{9}{20} \)
Equation: \( \frac{x}{6} = \frac{9}{20} \)
Multiply both sides by 6 (to clear denominator):
\( x = \frac{9}{20} \times 6 = \frac{54}{20} = \frac{27}{10} \)
2. \( \frac{n}{2} - \frac{3n}{4} + \frac{5n}{6} = 21 \)
Write with common denominator LCM = (12):

\( \frac{n}{2} \times \frac{6}{6} = \frac{6n}{12} \), \( \frac{3n}{4} \times \frac{3}{3}= \frac{9n}{12} \), \( \frac{5n}{6} \times \frac{2}{2}= \frac{10n}{12} \)

\( \frac{6n}{12} - \frac{9n}{12} + \frac{10n}{12} \)
Combine terms:
\( \frac{6n - 9n + 10n}{12} = \frac{7n}{12} = 21 \)
Multiply both sides by 12:
\( 7n = 21 \times 12 = 252 \)
Divide both sides by 7:
\( n = \frac{252}{7} = 36 \)
3. \( x+7-\frac{8x}{3} = \frac{17}{6}-\frac{5x}{2} \)
Transpose \( \frac{5x}{2} \) to LHS and \( 7 \) to RHS:
\( x - \frac{8x}{3} + \frac{5x}{2} = \frac{17}{6} - 7 \)
LCM for x terms = 6:
\( x = \frac{6x}{6} \), \( \frac{8x}{3} = \frac{16x}{6} \), \( \frac{5x}{2} = \frac{15x}{6} \)
Add/subtract x terms:
\( \frac{6x - 16x + 15x}{6} = \frac{5x}{6} \)
RHS: \( \frac{17}{6} - 7 = \frac{17 - 42}{6} = -\frac{25}{6} \)
Equation: \( \frac{5x}{6} = -\frac{25}{6} \)
Multiply both sides by 6:
\( 5x = -25 \implies x = -5 \)
4. \( \frac{x-5}{3} = \frac{x-3}{5} \)
Cross-multiply:
\( 5(x-5) = 3(x-3) \)
Expand:
\( 5x - 25 = 3x - 9 \)
Bring all x terms to one side:
\( 5x - 3x = -9 + 25 \implies 2x = 16 \implies x = 8 \)
5. \( \frac{3t-2}{4} - \frac{2t+3}{3} = \frac{2}{3} - t \)
Bring all terms to one side:
\( \frac{3t-2}{4} - \frac{2t+3}{3} + t - \frac{2}{3} = 0 \)
Write all terms over 12 (LCM):
\( \frac{3t-2}{4} = \frac{9t-6}{12} \), \( \frac{2t+3}{3} = \frac{8t+12}{12} \), \( t = \frac{12t}{12} \), \( \frac{2}{3} = \frac{8}{12} \)
Equation:
\( \frac{9t-6 - (8t+12) + 12t - 8}{12} = 0 \)
Combine terms:
\( 9t-6-8t-12+12t-8 = (9t-8t+12t) + (-6-12-8) = (13t) + (-26) \)
Equation:
\( \frac{13t - 26}{12} = 0 \implies 13t - 26 = 0 \implies t = 2 \)
6. \( m - \frac{m-1}{2} = 1 - \frac{m-2}{3} \)
Transpose \( \frac{m-2}{3} \) to LHS and \( m \) to RHS:
\( m - \frac{m-1}{2} + \frac{m-2}{3} = 1 \)
LCM = 6:
\( m = \frac{6m}{6} \), \( \frac{m-1}{2} = \frac{3m-3}{6} \), \( \frac{m-2}{3} = \frac{2m-4}{6} \)
Equation:
\( \frac{6m - (3m-3) + (2m-4)}{6} = 1 \)
Calculate numerator:
\( 6m - 3m + 3 + 2m - 4 = (6m-3m+2m) + (3-4) = (5m) + (-1) \)
So \( \frac{5m-1}{6} = 1 \)
Multiply both sides by 6:
\( 5m-1 = 6 \implies 5m = 7 \implies m = \frac{7}{5} \)
7. \( 3(t-3) = 5(2t+1) \)
Expand both sides:
\( 3t - 9 = 10t + 5 \)
Transpose 10t to LHS:
\( 3t - 10t = 5 + 9 \implies -7t = 14 \implies t = -2 \)
8. \( 15(y-4) - 2(y-9) + 5(y+6) = 0 \)
Expand:
\( 15y - 60 - 2y + 18 + 5y + 30 = 0 \)
Combine like terms:
\( (15y-2y+5y) + (-60 + 18 + 30) = 18y - 12 = 0 \)
So \( 18y = 12 \implies y = \frac{2}{3} \)
9. \( 3(5z-7) - 2(9z-11) = 4(8z-13) - 17 \)
Expand:
\( 15z - 21 - 18z + 22 = 32z - 52 - 17 \)
LHS: \( 15z - 18z = -3z \), \( -21 + 22 = 1 \); RHS: \( 32z - 69 \)
Equation: \( -3z + 1 = 32z - 69 \)
Transpose 32z and 1:
\( -3z - 32z = -69 - 1 \implies -35z = -70 \implies z = 2 \)
10. \( 0.25(4f-3) = 0.05(10f-9) \)
Expand both sides:
\( 0.25 \times 4f = 1f \), \( 0.25 \times -3 = -0.75 \)
\( 0.05 \times 10f = 0.5f \), \( 0.05 \times -9 = -0.45 \)
Equation:
\( 1f - 0.75 = 0.5f - 0.45 \)
Transpose 0.5f to LHS, -0.75 to RHS:
\( 1f - 0.5f = -0.45 + 0.75 \)
\(\implies 0.5f = 0.3 \implies f = 0.6 \)
