EXERCISE 2.1 - Step by Step Solutions

1. Solve: 3x = 2x + 18
Transpose 2x to the left:
3x - 2x = 18
x = 18
Check:
LHS: 3x = 3 × 18 = 54
RHS: 2x + 18 = 2 × 18 + 18 = 36 + 18 = 54
LHS = RHS ✔️
2. Solve: 5t - 3 = 3t - 5
Transpose 3t to left, -3 to right:
5t - 3t = -5 + 3
2t = -2
t = -1
Check:
LHS: 5(-1) - 3 = -5 - 3 = -8
RHS: 3(-1) - 5 = -3 - 5 = -8
LHS = RHS ✔️
3. Solve: 5x + 9 = 5 + 3x
Transpose 3x to left, 9 to right:
5x - 3x = 5 - 9
2x = -4
x = -2
Check:
LHS: 5×(-2) + 9 = -10 + 9 = -1
RHS: 5 + 3×(-2) = 5 - 6 = -1
LHS = RHS ✔️
4. Solve: 4z + 3 = 6 + 2z
Transpose 2z to left, 3 to right:
4z - 2z = 6 - 3
2z = 3
z = 1.5
Check:
LHS: 4×1.5 + 3 = 6 + 3 = 9
RHS: 6 + 2×1.5 = 6 + 3 = 9
LHS = RHS ✔️
5. Solve: 2x - 1 = 14 - x
Transpose -x to left, -1 to right:
2x + x = 14 + 1
3x = 15
x = 5
Check:
LHS: 2×5 - 1 = 10 - 1 = 9
RHS: 14 - 5 = 9
LHS = RHS ✔️
6. Solve: 8x + 4 = 3(x - 1) + 7
Expand:
8x + 4 = 3x - 3 + 7
Simplify right side:
8x + 4 = 3x + 4
Transpose 3x to left:
8x - 3x = 4 - 4
5x = 0
x = 0
Check:
LHS: 8×0 + 4 = 0 + 4 = 4
RHS: 3(0 - 1) + 7 = -3 + 7 = 4
LHS = RHS ✔️
7. Solve: 45 × (x + 10) = x
Expand:
45x + 8 = x
Transpose 4/5x to right:
8 = x - 45x
8 = x(1 - 45) = x × 15
x = 8 × 5 = 40
Check:
LHS: 4/5 × (40 + 10) = 4/5 × 50 = 40
RHS: x = 40
LHS = RHS ✔️
8. Solve: 2x3 + 1 = 7x15 + 3
Transpose 7x/15 to left, 1 to right:
2x/3 - 7x/15 = 3 - 1
Common denominator (15):
10x/15 - 7x/15 = 2
3x/15 = 2 → x/5 = 2
x = 10
Check:
LHS: 2×10/3 + 1 = 20/3 + 1 = 23/3
RHS: 7×10/15 + 3 = 70/15 + 3 = 14/3 + 3 = (14+9)/3 = 23/3
LHS = RHS ✔️
9. Solve: 2y + 53 = 263 - y
Transpose -y to left, 5/3 to right:
2y + y = 26/3 - 5/3
3y = 21/3 = 7
y = 7/3
Check:
LHS: 2×(7/3) + 5/3 = 14/3 + 5/3 = 19/3
RHS: 26/3 - 7/3 = 19/3
LHS = RHS ✔️
10. Solve: 3m = 5m - 85
Transpose 5m to left:
3m - 5m = -8/5
-2m = -8/5
m = (8/5) × (1/2) = 4/5
Check:
LHS: 3×(4/5) = 12/5
RHS: 5×(4/5) - 8/5 = 4 - 8/5 = (20 - 8)/5 = 12/5
LHS = RHS ✔️
